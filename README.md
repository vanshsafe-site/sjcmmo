# 🎮 JosephiteMMO

*A nostalgic online school where everyone hangs out together.*

---

## 📖 Overview

JosephiteMMO is a browser-based multiplayer social RPG inspired by retro Nintendo DS school games.

Players from anywhere in the world can instantly join the same shared school, walk around, chat, explore, roleplay, and participate in fun activities with other players.

There are **no accounts**, **no passwords**, and **no separate servers**. Players simply enter a nickname and join the world.

The goal is to recreate the feeling of roaming around a huge school campus with friends in a charming pixel-art environment.

# 👨‍🎓 Multiplayer

Every player joins the same campus.

Simply:

1. Enter a nickname.
2. Click **Join**.
3. Start exploring.

Players can:

* Walk together
* Chat
* React with emotes
* Hang out
* Explore the campus
* Participate in school events

# 🎯 Vision

JosephiteMMO is designed to be a relaxing online social experience where players can meet new people, revisit the feeling of school adventures, and enjoy a vibrant retro pixel world together.

Instead of focusing on competition, the game encourages exploration, creativity, cooperation, and fun interactions in a single shared campus.
